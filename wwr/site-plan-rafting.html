Overview

Purpose

It is time to expand our white water rafting business. We have reached a point where it is important to have a website for the business. With a website, we will be able to reach a much larger audience. We will be able to have digital bookings that will keep us organized and help eliminate overbookings. We will be able to accept payments through the website. The website will have a history of our business to show potential customers that we have a wealth of knowledge about rafting, and therefore help to ease some of their concerns. Through the website we can give our potential customers a virtual experience that will excite them and prepare them for their future trips. These things will help to grow our business and expand our exposure.

Audience

The customers that we are trying to reach come from a broad range of people. Our business is geared to give everyone a great experience; from families just looking for a fun vacation, to those who are quite experienced. Many families are looking for some adventure to add to their vacation or even to build their vacation around. We can provide that for them. We can also accommodate the more experienced person by supplying them with the correct equipment and the knowledge that they will need of the river. The best way to target both of these demographics will be to advertize through other outdoor companies that have a much broader customer base over many areas of the outdoor culture. The majority of our customer base will most likely gain access to our site though a mobile device. We need to accommodate them by having a mobile friendly website.
